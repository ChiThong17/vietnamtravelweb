Viet Nam

Vietnam is a beautiful country with an incredible cultural and historical heritage. From the ancient capital of Hanoi to the stunning beaches of Da Nang, there is no shortage of great places to visit in Vietnam. However, there are many more off-the-beaten-path destinations that are considered by the famous travel website The Travel as hidden gems in Vietnam waiting to be discovered by visitors.

Top Destinations

  1. Ha Long Bay

    Located in Quang Ninh Province, Ha Long Bay is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

  2. Hoi An

    Hoi An is known for its well-preserved architecture and lantern-lit streets.

  3. Sapa

    Sapa offers stunning rice terraces and mountain views.
